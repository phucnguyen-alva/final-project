Imaginary Authors

Read The Air is an imaginative story exhibit created by Alva Nguyen, inspired by the titles of the top seven scent stories by Imaginary Authors. Each narrative unfolds like a hidden note in a perfume, capturing the essence of mystery, memory, and emotion that fragrances evoke. This digital space also invites readers, writers, and perfume enthusiasts to journey through a collection of tales and letters, where every story carries a whiff of wonder.
